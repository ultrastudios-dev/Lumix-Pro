 LUMIX Pro 3D Showcase
**Visual Studio: Ultra Studios**

Project Overview
This project is a high fidelity 3D kinetic showcase for the Lumix Pro Headset series. It focuses on achieving a professional, manual focus aesthetic through optimized code.

Purpose & Motivation
The creation of **Lumix Pro 3D** serves two primary goals:
1. **Cinematic Precision:** To replicate the mechanical feel of a high end camera body and lens within a digital interface.
2. **Hardware Optimization:** To prove that professional grade 3D visuals can be rendered and optimized on legacy hardware ( Pentium / 2GB RAM)

Technical Focus
- **Kinetic Motion:** Seamless 360 degree product interaction.
- **Visual Grading:** Cinematic color palettes and lighting simulation.
- **Lightweight Engine:** Built for maximum performance on limited resources.
